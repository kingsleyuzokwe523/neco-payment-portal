NECO Payments Just Got Faster and Simpler.

How it works

NOTE: Payment item code is required for any NECO payment, this item code can be gotten from the NECO website (www.neco.gov.ng). Ensure to verify your payment before proceeding to pay and note that refunds are not applicable once payment is completed.

Select Payment Item

Select or search for a payment category or a payment item you want to make payment for i.e Correction of Name, Syllabus, Exam Bag e.t.c

Get Payment Item Code

Payment item code is required for any NECO payment, this item code can be gotten from the NECO website www.neco.gov.ng. Ensure to verify your payment before proceeding to Pay and note that Refund is not applicable once payment is completed.

Fill the Payment Form

Fill the form in the box to the right and proceed to payment

Select Mode of Payment

Select mode of payment i.e Bank branch payment or Debit card payment.

Confirm Your Payment

Go through the transaction breakdown, read and accept the disclaimer then click the confirm button to proceed

Evidence of Payment

Present Evidence of Payment to your nearest NECO office.
